Barangay Justice Study

Documented Conflict Cases in Libertad

A descriptive study examining the types, frequency, and resolution procedures of conflicts processed under the Katarungang Pambarangay system.

Official Records
Barangay case files only
Descriptive Study
No Surveys, Interviews, or Opinion-based Inputs
Resolution Methods
Mediation, Conciliation and Arbitration Procedures
Community Focus
Libertad, Misamis Oriental
This study examines conflict cases officially recorded in selected barangays of Libertad, Misamis Oriental using existing barangay documentation.

Conflicts within communities are a normal aspect of everyday social life and require effective barangay-level dispute resolution.
